Breadsticks / Pizza Crust

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Yeast
  • 1 1/2 c warm water
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 1/2 c flour
  • 1/2 c butter, garlic salt, parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Mix yeast, water, salt, and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes. Add the flour and knead for 3 minutes.
  2. Let rise for an additional 10 minutes. Melt butter in 9x13 pan in oven.
  3. Roll out the dough to fit in the pan and place in the pan.
  4. Cover with a cloth and let raise for 10 more minutes. Sprinkle with garlic salt, and parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake 350 for 20 minutes. Cut and serve.

Can make into pizza with pizza sauce, and toppings.

Grandma's Bread

Ingredients

  • 6+ c flour
  • 1/4 c oil
  • 1Tbsp salt
  • 2 1/2 c lukewarm water
  • 1/4 c sugar
  • 1Tbsp yeast

Directions

  1. Mix, and knead dough.
  2. Let raise 2 1/2 hours.
  3. Mold into 2 loaves.
  4. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes.
