Holiday White Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 package white cake mix (regular size)
  • 1-1/3 cups water
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup (8 ounces) sour cream
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 teaspoon clear vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract

Directions

  1. 1. In a large bowl, combine the first 10 ingredients; beat on low speed for 30 seconds. Beat on medium for 2 minutes. Pour into two greased and floured 9-in. round baking pans.
  2. Bake at 325Â° for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks to cool completely.

Grasshopper Brownies

Ingredients

    Brownie
  • 2 sticks butter or margarine
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 4 eggs
  • 3/4 cup cocoa
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 eggs
    • Topping
  • 1 (8 ounce) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon mint extract
  • 6 drops green food coloring
    • Frosting
  • 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 stick margarine (room temperature)
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • A little water
  • A few drops of almond extract

Directions

  1. Melt butter or margarine in a large bowl. Add vanilla and sugar. Stir in eggs one at a time, stirring each one until completely mixed. Add cocoa and mix well. Add flour, salt, and baking powder. Mix well. Pour into a greased 13x9x2 pan
  2. Beat sugar and cream cheese until smooth. Add egg, mint extract, and food coloring. Mix ingredients well. Swirl mixture throughout and on top of brownie mixture.
  3. Bake at 350 for approximately 35 minutes.
  4. After brownies have cooled, mix powdered sugar, margarine, cocoa, vanilla, milk, and water until well blended. Add a few drops of almond extract, mix well. Frost brownies, cut, and serve.
